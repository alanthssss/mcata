# Merge Catalyst — Trailer Storyboard (30–45 seconds)

---

## Overview

**Length:** ~38 seconds
**Tone:** Clear, confident, satisfying — show systems in motion, not just tiles.
**Music:** Builds from ambient + sparse percussion to a punchy, layered climax.
**Palette:** Dark background, warm tile glow, catalyst flashes.

---

## 0–3s — Title + Hook

**Visual:**
- Black screen fades in.
- Grid appears: 4×4 board, a few tiles visible (Iron, Bronze, Gold levels).
- Title card: **MERGE CATALYST**

**On-screen text:**
> "Merge tiles."

**What to record:**
- Static title card + logo reveal animation.
- Grid first-frame snapshot with a couple of mid-tier tiles.

---

## 3–10s — Grid + Merge + Simple Explanation

**Visual:**
- Player swipes left → 3 tiles slide and merge simultaneously.
- Score output flashes: **+40 Output** (chain ×1.5 glow)
- Small label: "Reach target Output before Steps run out."

**On-screen text:**
> "Slide. Merge. Score."
> "6 phases. Limited steps. Build your engine."

**What to record:**
- A clean 3-tile chain merge with output flash.
- Phase panel showing "Phase 1 / 6" and steps counting down.
- Output counter ticking up.

---

## 10–20s — Catalyst Effects + Synergy + Momentum

**Visual:**
- Forge screen opens. Player picks **Chain Reactor** (rare).
- Synergy hint glows: "⚡ Synergy with Echo Multiplier!"
- Player picks **Echo Multiplier** → synergy badge lights up: **Chain Echo ×1.4**

**Cut to gameplay:**
- Player chains 4 merges in one move.
- Multipliers stack on screen:
  - Chain ×1.5
  - Catalyst (Chain Reactor) ×1.4
  - Synergy (Chain Echo) ×1.4
  - Momentum 🔥 ×1.6
- Final output flash: **+380 Output**

**On-screen text:**
> "Catalysts change everything."
> "Build synergies. Stack multipliers."

**What to record:**
- Forge modal with catalyst selection and synergy hint.
- Chained multi-merge with all multiplier badges visible.
- Score counter erupting.

---

## 20–30s — Anomaly Pressure + Big Moments

**Visual:**
- Phase panel shows: "⚠ Collapse Field active"
- Board: tiles are tight, one collapses mid-move.
- Player queues **Pulse Boost** signal.
- On next move: ×2.0 signal flash → massive merge → **Phase Clear!** banner.

**Cut to:**
- Fast montage (3–4 clips, ~1s each):
  - Entropy Tax blocking a cell
  - Momentum bar hitting MAX (×2.0)
  - Shield absorbing a phase penalty
  - Diamond-tier tile merge

**On-screen text:**
> "Anomaly phases will test your build."
> "Survive. Adapt. Win."

**What to record:**
- Anomaly text on Phase panel.
- Signal queue UI + activation.
- Phase Clear banner.
- Momentum bar maxing out.

---

## 30–38s — Different Builds + Replay Hint

**Visual:**
- Split-screen or rapid cut:
  - Left: Corner build (Corner Crown + Gravity Well + Empty Amplifier) — board fills corners with high tiles.
  - Right: Economy build (Energy Loop + Rich Merge + Reserve Bank) — energy counter surging.

**Voiceover / title card:**
> "Every run. A different build."

**Final 3 seconds:**
- Logo on dark background: **MERGE CATALYST**
- Tagline beneath: *"System Reaction Roguelike"*
- Call to action: **"Play the Demo — Available Now"**

**What to record:**
- Two complete run setups with different catalyst sets visible.
- Final logo card with subtle grid animation in background.

## Suggested Capture Settings

- Resolution: 1920×1080 (or native window scaled up)
- Frame rate: 60fps
- Hide debug panels during capture
- Enable `TILE_DISPLAY_MODE: 'label+value'` for readable tile labels in trailer
